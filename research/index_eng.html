Research

Risk Management and Energy and Mineral Economics

  • Prediction of change of petroleum production in the world and strategy of Japan developing petroleum globally
  • Analysis of worldwide transportation system of natural gas and influence from changing the route in Northeastern Asia
  • Prediction of ultimately scale distribution of petroleum and gas field
  • Optimization of the schedule of developing a strip mine
  • Production and trading model of the global natural gas
  • Research related to coal saving which aims at a stable supply of coal
  • Analysis of stable supple of coal using system dynamics

Real Option Analysis and Project Evaluation

  • Real option evaluation concerning introduction time of solar power plant
  • Feasibility evaluation of producing bioethanol using a fallow field in Japan
  • Optimization strategy of a special alloy production business which is undercompetition but has the option to expand equipment
  • Evaluation of development strategy of two-phase stainless steel using improved multi-stage SSA

Social System Modeling

  • Global energy economy model aiming at evaluation of long-term growth scenario
  • Prediction of future market of electric cars taking consumers` preference into consideration
  • Oil prices' change model taking energy substitution and economic development into consideration

Evaluation of Policies Promoting Introduction of Natural Energy

  • Evaluation of self-distributed generation system which aims at large scale introduction of natural energy
  • Quantitative evaluation of policies introducing solar power generation using feed-in Tariff
  • Electricity supply strategy incorporating solar power generation associated with spread of electric cars
  • Evaluation of policies introducing solar power generation which takes characteristics of the region into consideration
